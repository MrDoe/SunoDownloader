# Privacy Policy - SunoDownloader

## Overview
SunoDownloader is a Firefox browser extension that helps users download their published Suno songs. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how our extension operates.

## Data Collection and Use

### No External Data Collection
- **SunoDownloader does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.**
- The extension only communicates with suno.com to access your publicly available songs that you have authorized access to.
- No data is sent to any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

### Local Storage Only
- Your download preferences and history are stored **locally on your device only**, using your browser's storage API.
- This data never leaves your computer and is not shared with anyone.

## Permissions Explanation

The extension requests the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| `downloads` | Required to download Suno songs to your device |
| `scripting` | Required to parse and extract song information from Suno's website |
| `activeTab` | Allows the extension to work on the current tab you're viewing |
| `storage` | Used to store your download preferences locally on your device |
| `*://*.suno.com/*` | Required to access your songs on suno.com |

## What We Don't Do
- ❌ We don't track your browsing behavior
- ❌ We don't create accounts or require authentication separate from Suno
- ❌ We don't collect device identifiers or fingerprinting data
- ❌ We don't sell or share your data
- ❌ We don't use cookies for tracking purposes
- ❌ We don't have a server infrastructure (open-source project)

## Your Data Security
Since all processing happens locally on your device:
- Your data remains under your control
- There are no external servers to be compromised
- Your Suno credentials are never accessed or stored by this extension

## Open Source
SunoDownloader is an open-source project. You can inspect the complete source code on [GitHub](https://github.com/MrDoe/SunoDownloader) to verify these claims.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy. Any changes will be reflected in updates to the extension.

## Questions?
For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/MrDoe/SunoDownloader).
